Beginning

How are you actually doing — across all of life?

Most tools track one thing. Life OS holds all seven. Path, Spark, Body, Finances, Relationships, Inner Game, Outer Game — seen together, honestly, as a whole.

This is not about optimization. It's about orientation.

Three questions. Seven domains. Your actual map.

In each area of life, you'll define what 10/10 looks like for someone like you specifically — then describe where you honestly are — then name your actual wish.

The scale is yours. The finish line is yours. The map is yours.

No right answers. Only honest ones.

This takes about 20–30 minutes. By the end, you'll know where you are, where you're going, and which three domains — if you focused on them — would move everything else.

The goal is a full yes life. Let's find out where you're starting from.
