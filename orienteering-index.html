Life OS: Orienteering

See yourself clearly. Orient wisely.

Beginning
The Map

Seven domains. One honest picture.

Path · Spark · Body · Finances · Relationships · Inner Game · Outer Game — rated honestly, seen as a whole.

Most people sense that something is off somewhere. Orienteering shows you exactly where.

The Method

One domain at a time. Your map builds as you go.

For each domain, you'll place yourself honestly on a scale — then name what 10/10 looks like for you.

The scale is yours. No right answers. Only honest ones.

The Outcome

About 15 minutes. A full picture of where you are.

By the end you'll know where you are across all seven domains, which areas are dragging everything else, and which three — if you focused on them — would move the most.

Navigate from clarity, not pressure.
